ensure-list

Pay attention! A very useful utility function is being introduced here.

> (ensure-list 'a)
(A)
> (ensure-list '(a))
(A)

Often used in looping and mapping to allow for handling both lists and singletons.

> (defun foo (arg)
    (mapc 'princ (ensure-list arg)))

> (foo 1)
1

> (foo '(2 3))
23
